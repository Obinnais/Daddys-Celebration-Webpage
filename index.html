The Life Of Bishop Dr. Victor Isiodu

In August 1996, The Lord gave this vessel release to formally resign what was considered a promising Pastorate in Deeper Life Bible Church in favour of Kingdom service.
The first step was to convene a meeting with Elders at the Gate of Owerri to share the reality of availability to serve the whole Body of Christ just as the Lord directed.

Dr Victor Isiodu was one of those Elders who we met at the Owerri Chamber of Commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture.
He was one of those who prayed for the Kingdom vision, opening the doors of the City. Something clicked at that meeting: love at first sight!
Over the 30 years since then, Dr Victor Isiodu has been consistent in a number of ways which mark him out as a pacesetter in his generation and for generations to come:

  1. His love and devotion to his dear wife, Pastor Grace and their dynamic children who were raised in the Lord for His glory.
  2. His love for the Body and understanding of the power of unity. We worked closely together with other leaders on the project to save PFN; Jesus Week,
    the 1.2 million Reinhard Bonnke Crusade; and bringing the Body of Christ together.
  3. His compassionate heart is evidenced in how he uses his medical profession to penetrate tough terrains in the gospel. He offers his services to rural communities without charging.
    My roots, Alumaku City in Uzoagba, Ikeduru has benefited multiple times.
  4. In Prayer and Spiritual Warfare, he has been consistently on the cutting edge of pushing back the frontier of darkness across Imo State and beyond.The Wednesday prayer meetings he convenes at BALM center off Onitsha Road is a spiritual powerhouse in this regard. Hosting of the Jubilee Service to mark 50 years of Imo State was a demonstration of this reality.
  5. In wisdom delivered with wit and cheerfulness, Dr Isiodu is just too much.
  6. His role in mentoring and affirming younger ministers is unparalleled with the emergence of Pastor Ikenna Agugua as the main networker in Owerri as a case in point.
  7. His humble disposition and relatable nature is evidence in our relationship. When we undertook an International Mission to the USA together 24 years ago. He never pulled the rank of being the senior (by 10 year and 8 months) but constantly affirmed me. We flew together into United Kingdom thereafter.
  8. His understanding of the redemptive purpose of Africa in the end time move of God is a joy to behold and hear. The revelation of the special place of Owerri in that regard is spot on.

In closing, the International Ministers Fellowship would like to offer Dr Isiodu a special honour as the Special Advisor to the International President on fulfilment of the Redemptive purpose of African.
We invite him to be part of the speakers at the General Convocation of the Remnant in Lusaka, Zambia by end of October 2026 to mark the 25th anniversary of International Ministers Fellowship.

Apostle George Akalonu
International President,
